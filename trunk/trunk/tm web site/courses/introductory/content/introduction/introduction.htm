Objectives

  1. Introduction to (program) design.
  2. Development of algorithmic problem solving skills.
  3. Fundamentals of computing.
  4. Fundamentals of programming.
  5. Introduction to C++ programming.

Group Work

Group work is not permitted. You must do your own assignments and any violation of this will be treated as plagiarism.

Due Date

All assignments are due at 11:59 pm the day after your lab. Late submissions are not accepted except by special permission of the lecturer.

Lab/Tutorials

Lab/tutorials are held in the main computer lab (EN3000). They are designed to help you work on your assignments. As they are only an hour long you are strongly advised to review and make a start on your assignment before going to the lab.
